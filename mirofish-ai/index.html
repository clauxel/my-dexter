MiroFish AI

MiroFish AI turns seed data into multi-agent prediction simulations.

Use this page when you want the short version of what MiroFish does: it builds a structured world from your input, lets many AI agents interact inside that world, and turns the result into a report you can question.

Learn how to use MiroFish Open MiroFish GitHub

Quick answer

MiroFish AI is best understood as a prediction and simulation engine, not a single prompt tool. You provide seed material and a scenario. MiroFish extracts entities, builds a graph, prepares simulated agents, runs interactions, and produces a structured report.

That makes it useful for questions where human behavior, public narratives, competing incentives, or group reactions matter more than a simple statistical forecast.

Best fit

  • Scenario planning
  • Public opinion analysis
  • Market narrative research
  • Policy and campaign rehearsal
  • Story-world and persona simulation

What makes it different from ordinary AI chat

Seed driven

MiroFish starts from documents, notes, or pasted context instead of relying only on a generic model memory.

Graph based

The system organizes entities and relationships before simulation, which helps keep a complex scenario coherent.

Agent based

Multiple generated agents interact over rounds, making it easier to inspect shifts, conflicts, and turning points.
