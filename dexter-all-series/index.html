Research Capabilities

Task Decomposition

Complex financial questions are broken into 2–6 targeted sub-tasks. Each sub-task is executed independently, then synthesized into a single report.

Self-Validation Loop

Dexter checks its own findings against multiple data sources before returning results. Inconsistencies are flagged rather than silently averaged.

Scratchpad Logging

Every research step is logged — tools called, data retrieved, validation checks run. Full audit trail available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

Natural Language Input

Ask questions in plain English. No query syntax, no financial jargon required — Dexter maps intent to structured research tasks automatically.

Structured Reports

Findings are returned in a consistent, readable format with key metrics, trend summaries, source citations, and confidence indicators.

Loop Detection

Built-in execution limits prevent runaway agent loops on ambiguous queries. Clear error messaging when a query exceeds resolution capacity.

Live Financial Data Sources

  • Income statements — Revenue, COGS, gross profit, operating income, net income (quarterly and annual)
  • Balance sheets — Assets, liabilities, equity, debt levels, working capital
  • Cash flow statements — Operating, investing, and financing cash flows; free cash flow
  • Market prices — Real-time and historical stock price data
  • Segment data — Business segment breakdowns where available
  • Financial ratios — P/E, EV/EBITDA, FCF yield, debt/equity, and more (computed from live data)
  • Web search — Supplementary context via Exa/Tavily for news, earnings call summaries, analyst commentary

Dexter TV Series: Complete Guide

Looking for the Dexter TV show? Here's a quick overview of all series:

  • Dexter (Seasons 1–8, 2006–2013) — The original Showtime series following Dexter Morgan, a Miami blood spatter analyst who leads a secret life as a vigilante serial killer. 96 episodes total.
  • Dexter: New Blood (2021–2022) — 10-episode limited series revival. Dexter is living quietly in Iron Lake, NY, until his past resurfaces.
  • Dexter: Original Sin (2024–2025) — Prequel series showing Dexter's early years in Miami Metro and the origins of his "Dark Passenger." Season 1 has 10 episodes.
  • Dexter: Resurrection (2025–) — Continuation following the events of New Blood. Season 1 premiered July 11, 2025, and season 2 has been ordered.

Paramount+ is the main streaming home for the Dexter universe in the U.S.; availability can vary by country, so check your local streaming app before subscribing.
