The Resurrection of Financial Research

For decades, financial research meant analysts manually pulling reports, building comparison models, and spending more time on data preparation than on insight. AI chatbots helped at the surface level — they could summarize, explain, and brainstorm — but they couldn't access live data or validate their own numbers.

Autonomous AI agents changed that. Systems like Dexter AI don't just answer from training knowledge; they actively retrieve, cross-check, and validate live financial data — then return structured research reports. The craft of financial analysis has been resurrected in a new form.

Dexter AI: The Platform Behind the Resurrection

Dexter AI is a hosted SaaS platform based on the open-source virattt/dexter project. It runs a full autonomous research pipeline on demand:

  • Submit any financial question in natural language
  • The agent decomposes it and plans a research strategy
  • Live data is retrieved from financial APIs
  • Self-validation loop ensures consistency
  • Structured report returned in under 3 minutes

Available on Pro and Enterprise plans: multi-LLM routing (Claude, GPT, Gemini, xAI), full financial statement access, API integration, and priority processing.

About Dexter: Resurrection (TV Series)

Dexter: Resurrection is the continuation of the Dexter television universe after Dexter: New Blood. Season 1 premiered on July 11, 2025, on Paramount+ with Showtime, with Michael C. Hall returning as Dexter Morgan.

The first season runs 10 episodes, and Showtime/Paramount+ announced that the series will continue with a second season.
