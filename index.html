Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dexter AI?
Dexter AI is an autonomous financial research agent. Ask any financial question — stocks, sectors, earnings, market trends — and Dexter breaks it into tasks, pulls live data, self-validates findings, and returns a structured report. It acts like a junior analyst available 24/7.
How is Dexter AI different from ChatGPT or Perplexity?
ChatGPT generates text from training data. Perplexity browses the web. Dexter actively queries structured financial data APIs — income statements, balance sheets, cash flows, real-time prices — then validates output through iterative cross-checking. It retrieves and verifies, not guesses.
Is the financial data real-time?
Yes. Dexter connects to live financial data providers for income statements, balance sheet data, cash flow statements, and market pricing. It does not rely on training-data knowledge for financial figures.
What AI models does Dexter use?
Pro and Enterprise plans support Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), Gemini (Google), and xAI. Dexter selects optimal model routing per research sub-task.
Can I start with the middle plan?
Yes. Pro is selected by default because it includes unlimited research, API access, and priority processing. Annual billing is selected by default because it saves 50% compared with monthly billing.
How does the annual discount work?
Choosing annual billing saves you 50% compared to monthly. The discount is applied automatically when you select annual at checkout. You can switch back to monthly at renewal.
